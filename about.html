01 Mission

Our Mission

Follow The Lacrosse is dedicated to growing the sport of lacrosse across Europe by providing comprehensive coverage, fostering community engagement, and connecting players, coaches, and fans with the latest developments in European lacrosse.

📢

Inform

Provide accurate, timely news and analysis about European lacrosse events and developments.

👥

Connect

Build bridges between lacrosse communities across different European countries and regions.

📈

Grow

Support the expansion and development of lacrosse throughout Europe at all levels.
